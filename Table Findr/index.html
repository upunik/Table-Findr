Welcome to Table Findr

Have you ever had any difficulties finding a relaxing place to study?

Table Findr covers these main issues that students face by providing students with an opportunity to use tables at restaurants to study, talk out loud and eat. Restaurants have the opportunity of using Table Findr for opening up a selected number of tables that are available for students to come and study. Students can use Table Findr to locate a restaurant that has open tables so that they can study in groups and also have the freedom to eat while studying. It’s an advantage for both parties, because during a restaurant’s slow hours such as the mornings and late afternoons, they have the opportunity to open up tables just for students who can come in, study and potentially purchase from the restaurant itself. It helps the restaurant’s business during slow hours while also giving students the benefit of easily locating a place to study, talk and eat.
